About Us

Hi! We're Kyle and Paula, two millenials learning to garden through trial, error, and the internet. We moved to Wisconsin together in 2020 and got our first yard. Like lots of people, we picked up gardening when the world shut down. Turns out, we really like it! We're learning together as we transform our patch of grass into an urban oasis.
